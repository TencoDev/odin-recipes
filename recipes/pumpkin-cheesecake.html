Pumpkin Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • ½ cup pumpkin puree
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground cloves, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg, or more to taste

Steps to make a perfect Pumpkin Cheesecake

  1. Gather the ingredients and preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, blending well after each addition.
  4. Spread 1 cup of the cheesecake batter in the graham cracker crust.
  5. Add pumpkin puree, cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg to the remaining batter; stir gently until well blended.
  6. Carefully spread the pumpkin batter on top of the plain cheesecake batter in the crust.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until the edges are puffed and the surface is firm except for a small spot in the center that jiggles when the pan is gently shaken.
  8. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and set it on a wire rack to cool to room temperature, 1 to 2 hours.
  9. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 3 hours before serving, preferably overnight.
  10. Serve the cheesecake, topped with whipped cream if desired.
